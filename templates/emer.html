E lock

Emergency lock (E lock) will lock you vechile completely. You cannot unlock your bike using the keys or fingerprint or by any circuit bypassing techniques. You can only unlock the bike if E lock is OFF.

E-lock status

Working

We have made our device connection in such a way that the engine would start only if our micro-controller commands to start. In Elock mode the device will not send respond to start sequences, so to unlock your bike make sure to turn-off Elock
